Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about NutriScan

How does the product analyze food from just a barcode?

When you scan a barcode, our system fetches the nutrition facts from verified databases. Then, AI and ML models interpret the data, compute GI, NutriScore, and EcoScore, and convert raw numbers into meaningful health insights in seconds.

Can it help people with diabetes or other conditions?

Yes. The system flags high sugar or carbohydrate risks instantly, making it especially useful for diabetes management. It also adapts recommendations to your profile (age, health conditions, and preferences) for safer and more relevant guidance.

What makes this different from normal nutrition apps?

Most apps just show calories and nutrients. Our product explains what those numbers mean for your health, gives a clear Good/OK/Bad label, and offers 5–6 practical tips you can use right away — plus an education module to help you learn.

Is it available in local languages?

Yes. All recommendations are delivered in English, Hindi, and Bengali — both as text and natural TTS (voice). This makes the product accessible to a wider audience, from urban shoppers to community health programs.

Do I need an internet connection to use it?

No, the app works in both online and offline modes. You can scan and analyze foods offline using cached nutrition data and built-in AI rules, while the online mode ensures real-time updates, latest product info, and caregiver alerts.
