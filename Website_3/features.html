NutriScan Features

NutriScan transforms a simple barcode scan into instant, trustworthy, and actionable nutrition guidance. Discover how our AI-powered features can help you make healthier food choices.

Instant Nutrient Analysis

Scan any packaged food barcode and immediately receive a complete breakdown of its nutritional content, including protein, carbohydrates, fats, sugars, and fiber.

Our AI algorithms analyze this data to provide you with visual scorecards that make it easy to understand the nutritional value at a glance.

  • Comprehensive nutrient breakdown
  • Glycemic Index (GI) calculation
  • NutriScore rating (A to E)
  • EcoScore for environmental impact
  • Clear health label (Good / OK / Bad)

Core Features

Personalized Recommendations

Receive 5-6 tailored dietary tips for each product based on your age, health status, and dietary preferences. Our AI considers your specific needs to provide the most relevant advice.

Multilingual Support

Access information in both text and voice format in multiple languages including English, Hindi, and Bengali, making nutrition information accessible to diverse users.

Explainable AI

Understand why each recommendation was made through our Education Module, which provides transparent rule traces and short educational notes for each suggestion.

Care Mode

Enable caregivers to monitor scan history remotely and receive instant safety alerts, making it easier to support family members with specific dietary needs.

Health Risk Detection

Automatically flag diabetes-related risks by detecting high sugar/carbohydrate patterns and highlighting immediate concerns based on your health profile.

Customizable Profiles

Configure your personal profile with age, known health conditions (e.g., diabetes), and dietary preferences to receive the most relevant recommendations.

Multilingual Text and Voice

NutriScan breaks language barriers by providing nutrition information in multiple languages, making healthy eating accessible to everyone.

Our natural Text-to-Speech (TTS) technology converts recommendations into clear voice guidance in English, Hindi, and Bengali, helping users who prefer auditory learning or have visual impairments.

  • Text display in multiple languages
  • Natural-sounding voice output
  • Easy language switching
  • Accessibility for diverse users
  • Support for regional dialects

Care Mode for Guardians

Care Mode enables family members and caregivers to monitor the food choices of their loved ones remotely, providing peace of mind and support.

Guardians receive instant alerts when potentially problematic foods are scanned, allowing for timely intervention and guidance.

  • Shared scan history between users
  • Real-time safety alerts
  • Remote monitoring capabilities
  • Customizable alert thresholds
  • Privacy controls for shared information

Technical Features

Comprehensive Food Database

Access information on millions of food products with regularly updated nutritional data to ensure accuracy and relevance.

Real-time Processing

Get instant results with our optimized algorithms that process nutrition data and generate recommendations in seconds.

Cloud Synchronization

Access your scan history and preferences across multiple devices with secure cloud synchronization.

Data Privacy

Rest assured that your personal health data and scan history are protected with industry-standard encryption and privacy controls.
