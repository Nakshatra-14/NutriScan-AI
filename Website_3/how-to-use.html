How to Use NutriScan

1

Create Your Account

Start by creating a personal account to get recommendations tailored to your health profile.

Click on the "Login" button in the top navigation bar and select "Register" to create a new account. Fill in your details including age, any health conditions (like diabetes), and dietary preferences.

2

Access the Barcode Scanner

Once logged in, navigate to the "Scan Barcode" section from the navigation menu.

This will open the camera interface where you can scan product barcodes. Make sure to grant camera permissions when prompted.

3

Scan a Product

Hold your device so that the barcode is clearly visible in the camera frame. The app will automatically detect and scan the barcode.

Keep the barcode steady and ensure there's good lighting for faster detection. The scanner works with all standard product barcodes found on packaged foods.

4

Review Nutrition Information

After scanning, you'll see a complete breakdown of the product's nutrition facts, including:

  • Protein, carbohydrates, fats, sugars, and fiber content
  • Glycemic Index (GI) score
  • NutriScore rating (A to E)
  • EcoScore for environmental impact
  • Overall health label (Good / OK / Bad)
5

Get Personalized Recommendations

Below the nutrition information, you'll find 5-6 personalized recommendations based on the product and your health profile.

These tips are tailored to your age, health conditions, and dietary preferences to help you make better food choices.

6

Use Multilingual Features

NutriScan supports multiple languages including English, Hindi, and Bengali. You can switch languages from the settings menu.

You can also enable text-to-speech to have the information read aloud in your preferred language.

Pro Tips for Better Results

  • Ensure good lighting when scanning barcodes for faster detection.
  • Update your health profile regularly for more accurate recommendations.
  • Use the "Care Mode" feature to monitor family members' food choices remotely.
  • Check the "Education Module" to understand why certain recommendations are made.
  • Save frequently scanned items to your favorites for quick access.
  • Compare similar products to make the healthiest choice.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the nutrition information?
NutriScan uses a comprehensive database of food products that is regularly updated. The nutrition information is sourced directly from manufacturer data and verified by our nutrition experts.
What is the Glycemic Index (GI) and why is it important?
The Glycemic Index measures how quickly a food raises blood sugar levels. Foods with a high GI are rapidly digested and cause significant fluctuations in blood sugar, while low GI foods are digested more slowly, causing a gradual rise in blood sugar. This is particularly important for people with diabetes or those managing their weight.
How does the Care Mode work?
Care Mode allows designated caregivers to monitor the scan history and receive alerts when certain products are scanned. To enable this feature, go to Settings > Care Mode and add the email address of the caregiver you want to share information with.
Can I use NutriScan offline?
NutriScan requires an internet connection to access the product database and provide personalized recommendations. However, previously scanned items are stored locally and can be viewed offline.
How do I report a product that's not in the database?
If a product is not recognized, you can report it by selecting "Product Not Found" after scanning and filling out the product information form. Our team will add it to the database as soon as possible.
